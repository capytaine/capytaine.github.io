  • The complex-valued amplitudes (phasors) are defined with the convention \(x(t) = \Re ( X e^{-i \omega t})\). It is similar to Nemoh and unlike WAMIT in which the convention \(x(t) = \Re ( X e^{+ i \omega t})\) is used.

  • Unlike most other codes, angles (such as the incoming wave direction) are given in radians.
